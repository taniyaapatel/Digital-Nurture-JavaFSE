Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?
Navigate to the Register section on the main page, fill out the form with your name, email, date, selected event, and optionally feedback. Click submit and click "OK" on the browser confirmation prompt.
Why does the portal ask for my location?
The portal uses your browser's geolocation API to calculate the coordinates and find community centers and events closest to you. We do not store or track this location data.
What are event fees and how are they determined?
Different events have different registration fees based on equipment and hosting costs. Selecting an event type on the registration form will automatically update and display the corresponding fee.
How can I clear my saved preferences?
We store your preferred event selection locally so you do not have to select it again. To clear all stored preferences and cache, click the "Clear Preferences" button next to the registration form.
