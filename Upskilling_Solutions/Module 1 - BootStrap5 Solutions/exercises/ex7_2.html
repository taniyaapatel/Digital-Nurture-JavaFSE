Text Case Transformations

Using utilities to manage text capitalization styles dynamically.

text-uppercase

attention: all volunteers must wear protective safety gear at the garden cleanup site.

Forces all characters to capital letters.

text-lowercase

INFO@GREENWOODHUB.ORG - PLEASE DIRECT ALL EMAIL NOTICES TO THIS OFFICIAL ADDRESS.

Forces all characters to lowercase letters.

text-capitalize

john doe - lead environmental coordinator of local park services.

Capitalizes the first letter of each word.
