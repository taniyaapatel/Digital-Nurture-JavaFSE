Community Garden Volunteers Needed

Posted on May 28, 2026 by Greenwood Green Committee

We are looking for passionate neighbors to help plant summer vegetables, maintain paths, and build new compost bins this weekend at the Greenwood community garden.

No prior gardening experience is required! Tools, sunscreen, and light refreshments will be provided for all volunteers. Please meet at the community center greenhouse by 9:00 AM on Saturday.
