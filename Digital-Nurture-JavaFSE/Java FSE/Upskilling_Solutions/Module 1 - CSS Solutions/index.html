A Message from the Town Council

Published by Council Secretary on May 28, 2026

Dear neighbors, we are excited to announce several new initiatives starting this summer season. First, our park renovation projects are officially underway. We are upgrading the playground equipment at Oakwood Park and installing new benches along the walking trails. We hope these updates make our parks more accessible and enjoyable for families and seniors alike. We ask for your patience during the construction phases as certain areas of the park may be temporarily cordoned off for safety reasons.

In addition to park upgrades, we are partnering with the county to host monthly recycling clinics. These clinics will educate households on proper waste sorting techniques and provide resources for disposing of electronics and hazardous chemicals safely. Proper waste management is critical to keeping Greenwood clean and protecting our local waterways and wildlife.

Lastly, we encourage everyone to join our upcoming town hall meetings. These meetings occur on the first Tuesday of every month at the Main Hall. Your voices and opinions are vital as we shape the future development plans of our community. We look forward to hearing your ideas, addressing your concerns, and working together to keep Greenwood a wonderful place to live, work, and grow.
